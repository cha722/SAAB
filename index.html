About Saab Petroleum

Saab Petroleum is a Lebanon-based, full-service petroleum company founded in 2020. We are committed to providing quality fuel and exceptional service to our customers across Beirut.

With our 24/7 fuel stations in Badaro, Hamra, and Zaidaniyeh, we ensure that you never run out of fuel when you need it most. Our mission is to fuel Lebanon's progress with reliability and excellence.
